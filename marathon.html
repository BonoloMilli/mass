Double Green

A five-concept kit pack for Comrades Marathon called “Double Green.” The palette is drawn from the Lesotho flag, honouring heritage and making the athlete instantly recognisable on race day.

Challenge & Solution

Challenge

Build a kit that is high-visibility at speed, photogenic for broadcast, and simple for production—while reflecting Lesotho identity without copying the flag verbatim.

Approach

  • Locked a two-green core with blue/white/black accents for contrast and practicality.
  • Reserved the chest for race bib/sponsor lockups; used white panels for legibility.
  • Explored five looks (Designs 1–5): from clean heritage stripes to diagonal sash and tonal geometric greens—each balancing culture, performance, and sponsor clarity.
  • Delivered vector masters, colour call-outs, and print zones for sublimation/heat-press.

Colour & symbolism

  • Double Green (#009A44 & #006C3B): land, growth, endurance; two tones create depth on fabric and movement on camera.
  • Sky Blue (#0050F0): clarity and ambition; used as trim/piping and number-field accents.
  • White (#FFFFFF): clean panels for bib/sponsor readability and heat relief.
  • Black (#000000): sharp outlines and micro-motifs, a subtle nod to the Mokorotlo emblem.

Final Result

A cohesive, flag-informed athlete identity that reads instantly as Lesotho-proud, is easy to manufacture, and stays consistent across vest, tee, shorts, and cap—ready for one hero race kit plus alternates for training and partner activations.
