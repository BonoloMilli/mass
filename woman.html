Chaimlos Women

Chaimlos FC launched a women’s side to create a structured space for women in football and fitness in Sandton. We developed the visual identity direction, a prism-inspired kit pattern, and a scalable graphic system that works across kits, social graphics, and event collateral.

Challenge & Solution

Challenge

Make the women’s team look elite yet community-led; ensure assets adapt from jersey print to digital posts.

Approach

  • Pattern and typography system designed for legibility on fabric and impact online.
  • Social templates and matchday graphics to keep the feed cohesive.
  • Guidance for future youth pathways and partnerships.

Final Result

A cohesive look and toolkit that elevates the women’s programme, attracts players/partners, and keeps content consistent across platforms.
